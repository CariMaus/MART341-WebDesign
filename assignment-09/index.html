Items You Will Need

  • Cooking spray
  • Small frying pan
  • Spatula
  • 1-3 eggs
  • 1-2 Tble butter
  • Splash of milk
  • Shredded cheese (if desired)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Srambled Eggs Instructable

Step 1 - Egg Preparation

Crack 1-3 eggs into a bowl. Add milk. Whisk eggs and milk in bowl with fork until frothy.

Step 2 - Pan and Stove preparation

Spray frying pan with cooking spray. Turn on stove top burner to medium/low. Place greased pan on burner. Add butter to frying pan, wait until fully metlted to add eggs.

Step 3 - Cooking Eggs

Pour whisked eggs into frying pan. Scramble eggs every 3 seconds. If pan seems too hot, turn down heat. Low heat is best to not burn eggs. Scramble often. Add cheese before eggs become firm.
