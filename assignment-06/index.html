index.html Gluten Free Banana Bread <h1> Ingredients </h1> <ul> <li> <b>2-3</b> very <i>ripe</i> bananas mashed </li> <li> <b>1/3</b> cup melted unsalted butter </li> <li> <b>1</b> teaspoon baking soda </li> <li> <b>1 1/2</b> cup GF flour </li> <li> pinch of salt </li> <li> <b>3/4</b> cup granulated sugar </li> <li> <b>2</b> large eggs beaten </li> <li> <b>1</b> teaspoon vanilla extract </li> <h2> Directions </h2> <ol> <li> Mash bananas until smooth </li> <li> Add baking soda </li> <li> Stir in melted butter </li> <li> Stir in sugar, salt, eggs <i>(beaten)</i>, and vanilla </li> <li> Mix in GF flour </li> <li> Pour batter into greased pan </li> <li> Bake for 50-60 minutes at <b>350F</b> </li> <p><a href="https://www.mamaknowsglutenfree.com/easy-gluten-free-banana-bread/"> Original Recipe</a></p> <p><h3> Contact Me </h3></p> <p><a href="cari.maus@umontana.edu"> Cari Maus E-mail</a></p> <p>University of Montana</p> <address> <a href= "32 Campus Drive Missoula, MT"</a> </address> <!-- This is the best GF recipe ever. --> <<img src="" alt=""> https://www.mamaknowsglutenfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/gluten-free-banana-bread-1a.jpg
